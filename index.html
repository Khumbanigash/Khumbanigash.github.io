No matter what decisions we make, we will always have our minds, our thoughts, our opinions, our truths.

03 Decemeber 2021

I did a proposal to BP on 27 Novemeber 2021 at Matusadona Guest Lodge in Assagay/Hillcrest. I took her to do an Escape room in uMhlanga, then lunch at 1904 Bistro Avenue in Kloof, before the proposal. The proposal happened at 1700. There were candles on the floor and balloons. We stayed the night at the guest lodge. I fell asleep and BP did not sleep at all. I woke up after midnight and we did some fecks. We also talked. I fell asleep again. Then we had complimentary breakfast. They served us musueli with yoghurt, egss, toast, croissant, coffee, juice, and tomatos with melted cheese on it. The breakfast was tasty and well prepared. BP and I cried during the proposal. The first words she said were, "I knew it!". It was very difficult keeping the secret from her. I came clean about all the lies on the day. I regret not accepting Anne, the manager of the lodge, offer to record the proposal. I also missed her message of having a bon fire set up because her chat was set to silent. Oh well. I will definitely take her back there and maybe then we can have a bon fire. It was a very rushed job though. It happened very quickly. But it was still great. The families are pressuring us to do a wedding but we want to save for a home and BP just does not want to go through the whole process of actually planning and partaking in a wedding.
